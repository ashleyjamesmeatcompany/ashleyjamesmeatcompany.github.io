Our Story

Ashley James Meat Company was born out of a genuine passion and enjoyment in bringing the finest meat London has to offer, down to the south coast.

We make life difficult for ourselves, to bring the best to you. We work closely with chefs to get a better product onto their menu. Looking after you and your customers is our number one priority.

Our dedication to provide the best quality produce and the customer service to match has always been at the heart of the business right from the start.

Supplying fresh produce is what we pride ourselves in, no stock is carried, and everything is purchased to order from Smithfield Meat Market in London. We guarantee fresh meat everytime.
